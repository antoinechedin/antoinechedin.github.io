FROM THE SHADOWS

From the Shadows is a cooperative puzzle platformer game where players have to work their way through a mysterious mansion by resolving puzzles that revolve around the mechanics of Light and Shadow.

This game is an end of studies project for the Gamagora Game Show. It was made in 4 months between January and May 2020, with 2 months under quarantine due to COVID-19 that brought an additional challenge. We were a team of 18 students including programmers, level designers, 3D artists, and composers.

I was both lead developer and project manager. I was in charge of the character controller, and I created various tools for the game design, such as an automatic camera boundaries system, or customs UI components.

We're all very proud of our work, so don't hesitate to grab the game for free on Itch.io (link bellow)
