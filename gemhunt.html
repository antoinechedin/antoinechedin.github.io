GEM HUNT

Gem Hunt is a Steven Universe fan game I entirely made during summer 2019 on Unity. It's a strategic dice game inspired by Zombie dice where you hunt corrupted gems to earn points. Up to 5 player with friends or against the AI the same device. The game is playable on computer but also on smartphones, and is available in English, French, Spanish and Portuguese.
